# Citation Styles and Formats

## Academic Citation Styles

### APA (American Psychological Association)
- Used in: Social sciences, education, psychology
- Format: Author-date system
- Example: (Smith, 2023, p. 45)

### MLA (Modern Language Association)
- Used in: Humanities, literature, arts
- Format: Author-page system
- Example: (Smith 45)

### Chicago Manual of Style
- Used in: History, business, fine arts
- Format: Notes and bibliography or author-date
- Example: (Smith 2023, 45) or footnoteÂ¹

### IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers)
- Used in: Engineering, computer science, IT
- Format: Numeric system
- Example: [1]

### Harvard Style
- Used in: Social sciences, business
- Format: Author-date system
- Example: (Smith 2023)

## Digital Source Citations

### Web Pages
- Author, A. (Year). *Title of page*. Site Name. URL
- Access date included if content may change

### Online Articles
- Author, A. (Year, Month Day). Title of article. *Publication Name*. URL

### Social Media Posts
- Author [@username]. (Year, Month Day). *Content of post* [Post type]. Platform. URL

### Blog Posts
- Author, A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of blog post*. Blog Name. URL

## Data and Statistics

### Datasets
- Author/Organization. (Year). *Title of dataset* [Data set]. Publisher. DOI/URL

### Statistical Reports
- Organization. (Year). *Title of report*. Report number. Publisher. URL

### Survey Results
- Author. (Year). *Title of survey*. Survey company. URL

## Media Citations

### Images
- Creator. (Year). *Title of work* [Format]. Collection/Museum. URL

### Videos
- Creator. (Year). *Title of video* [Video]. Platform. URL

### Podcasts
- Host. (Year). *Title of episode* [Audio podcast]. Podcast Name. URL

## In-Text Citation Rules

### Direct Quotes
- Include page number: (Smith, 2023, p. 45)
- Use quotation marks: "direct quote"
- Keep quotes under 40 words for APA

### Paraphrasing
- No page number needed: (Smith, 2023)
- Must be significantly different wording
- Still requires citation

### Multiple Authors
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2023)
- 3-5 authors: (Smith et al., 2023)
- 6+ authors: (Smith et al., 2023)

### Multiple Sources
- Alphabetical order: (Jones, 2022; Smith, 2023)
- Semicolon separation
- Same author: (Smith, 2022, 2023)

## Reference List Formatting

### General Rules
- Alphabetical by author
- Hanging indent (first line flush left)
- Double spacing
- Consistent formatting

### Electronic Sources
- Include DOI when available
- Use stable URLs
- Include access date for dynamic content
- Note format type [e.g., PDF, HTML]

## Plagiarism Prevention

### Best Practices
- Always cite sources
- Use quotation marks for direct quotes
- Paraphrase correctly
- Keep good research notes

### Common Mistakes
- Forgetting to cite paraphrased content
- Incorrect citation formatting
- Missing reference list entries
- Self-plagiarism concerns
