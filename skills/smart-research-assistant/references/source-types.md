# Source Types and Classification

## Academic Sources

### Primary Sources
- Original research articles
- Conference proceedings
- Technical reports
- Patent documents
- Dissertations and theses

### Secondary Sources
- Review articles
- Meta-analyses
- Systematic reviews
- Academic books
- Reference works

### Tertiary Sources
- Encyclopedias
- Handbooks
- Dictionaries
- Bibliographies
- Indexes and abstracts

## Professional Sources

### Industry Reports
- Market research reports
- Industry analysis
- Company annual reports
- White papers
- Case studies

### Government Sources
- Official statistics
- Policy documents
- Regulatory filings
- Congressional reports
- Agency publications

### NGO Sources
- Advocacy reports
- Research publications
- Position papers
- Campaign materials
- Annual reports

## Media Sources

### Traditional Media
- Newspapers (broadsheet, tabloid)
- Magazines (news, business, trade)
- Television news
- Radio programs
- News agencies

### Digital Media
- Online news sites
- Digital magazines
- Podcasts
- Video platforms
- Social media

### Alternative Media
- Independent journalism
- Citizen reporting
- Blog networks
- Community media
- Niche publications

## Social Media Sources

### Platforms
- Twitter/X
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- Instagram
- TikTok
- Reddit
- YouTube

### Content Types
- Posts and updates
- Comments and discussions
- Live streams
- Stories and reels
- Groups and communities

## Evaluation Criteria by Source Type

### Academic Sources
- Peer review status
- Journal reputation
- Citation metrics
- Author credentials
- Methodology rigor

### Professional Sources
- Industry expertise
- Data transparency
- Methodology disclosure
- Conflict of interest
- Track record

### Media Sources
- Editorial standards
- Journalistic ethics
- Source diversity
- Correction policies
- Reputation metrics

### Social Media Sources
- Account verification
- Follower authenticity
- Engagement patterns
- Content consistency
- Community response

## Source Reliability Matrix

| Source Type | Reliability | Bias Risk | Update Frequency | Verification Difficulty |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|------------------|----------------------|
| Peer-reviewed journals | High | Low | Monthly | Medium |
| Government reports | High | Medium | Annual | Low |
| Major newspapers | Medium | Medium | Daily | Low |
| Industry reports | Medium | High | Quarterly | High |
| Social media | Low | High | Real-time | Very High |

## Special Considerations

### Language Barriers
- Translation accuracy
- Cultural context
- Regional differences
- Local terminology

### Access Issues
- Paywalls and subscriptions
- Geographic restrictions
- Platform limitations
- Technical requirements

### Timeliness Factors
- Breaking news vs. verified reports
- Preprint publications
- Embargo periods
- Scheduled releases
