## Brand Safety

Bundled styles may reference well-known products as inspiration labels or source identifiers. They are not official brand systems, do not grant trademark rights, and should not be used to copy logos, proprietary assets, screenshots, official claims, or brand identities.

When using this skill, describe outputs as "inspired by publicly visible UI patterns" or "style direction", not as official systems or replicas.
