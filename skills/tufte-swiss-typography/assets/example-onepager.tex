\BriefTitle{Q3 Pipeline Assessment}
\BriefSubtitle{CONFIDENTIAL --- EXECUTIVE SUMMARY}

\BriefMeta{PREPARED FOR}{Board of Directors}
\BriefMeta{DATE}{March 18, 2026}
\BriefMeta{CLASSIFICATION}{Internal Use Only}

\BriefSection{SITUATION}

The oncology pipeline entered Q3 with three Phase~II assets and one
Phase~III registrational study. Enrollment velocity for the lead asset
(RVN-4012) exceeded projections by 18\%, driven by site activation in
the EU-5. The competitive landscape shifted when Vertex disclosed
positive interim data in the same indication, compressing our window
for differentiation from 24 to approximately 14 months.

\BriefSection{KEY FINDINGS}

\begin{itemize}[
  label=\textcolor{TSMuted}{--},
  leftmargin=12pt,
  itemsep=4pt,
  topsep=\TSHalf,
  parsep=0pt,
]
\item RVN-4012 Phase~III enrollment at 73\% (target: 65\%). Data readout
  remains on track for Q1 2027.
\item RVN-5108 Phase~II futility analysis passed all pre-specified
  thresholds. Recommend expansion cohort activation.
\item RVN-3201 showed dose-limiting toxicity at the 400\,mg level. Protocol
  amendment to add a 300\,mg arm is in preparation.
\item Regulatory pre-submission meeting with FDA scheduled for October 2026.
  Breakthrough Therapy Designation request under internal review.
\end{itemize}

\BriefSection{RECOMMENDATION}

Accelerate the RVN-4012 regulatory timeline by six weeks. The Vertex
disclosure changes the competitive calculus: first-to-file advantage in
the EU is now worth an estimated \$180M in peak-year revenue. This
requires committing the reserve CMC budget (\$4.2M) to parallel-track
the commercial-scale manufacturing validation that would otherwise
begin in Q4.

The risk is bounded: if the Phase~III misses its primary endpoint,
the CMC investment is recoverable through contract manufacturing
partnerships already in negotiation.
