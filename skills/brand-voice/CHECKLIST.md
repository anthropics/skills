# Brand Voice Final Checklist

Claude must silently run through this checklist before returning any edited or generated content.
Only surface issues to the creator if something fails.

---

## Voice Consistency

- [ ] Does this sound like the creator's defined tone adjectives?
- [ ] Would the creator's regular audience immediately recognise this as theirs?
- [ ] Have all banned words and phrases been removed?
- [ ] Are the creator's signature phrases and habits preserved?
- [ ] Is the formality level correct for this creator's voice?

## Clarity

- [ ] Is the most important point front-loaded?
- [ ] Are there any sentences that could be split or shortened?
- [ ] Is there any repetition of the same idea in different words?
- [ ] Has all throat-clearing (unnecessary openers) been removed?
- [ ] Is every adjective earning its place, or is it filler?

## Format Fit

- [ ] Is the format right for the platform? (Tweet vs newsletter vs caption)
- [ ] Is the paragraph length appropriate for online reading?
- [ ] Does the ending give the reader a clear next feeling or action?

## Authenticity

- [ ] Does this sound like a human wrote it?
- [ ] Have all AI-default phrases been removed? (dive into, it's worth noting, in conclusion, etc.)
- [ ] Is there at least one specific detail, example, or observation — not just abstract claims?
- [ ] Does the content avoid empty affirmations or performative positivity?

## Final Check

- [ ] Read it aloud (mentally). Does it flow?
- [ ] Would the creator be proud to publish this as-is?

---

If any item fails, fix it before returning output. Do not mention this checklist to the creator
unless they ask how the review process works.
