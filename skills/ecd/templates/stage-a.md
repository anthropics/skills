# Stage A Template

## Goal

Extract the real goal from an unreliable, incomplete request by aggressively eliciting hidden meaning before approval.

## Must Capture

- ambiguity points
- dubious claims
- factual gaps
- hidden assumptions
- suspected real goals
- examples and counterexamples worth clarifying
- priority signals and tradeoff preferences
- unacceptable outcomes or anti-goals
- follow-up questions
- reframed request
