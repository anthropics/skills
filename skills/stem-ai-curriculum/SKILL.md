# STEM & AI Curriculum

Use this skill for STEM units, AI integration, inquiry design, or computational thinking tasks.

## Steps
1. Identify STEM concepts and real-world applications.
2. Integrate AI tools, data literacy, and computational thinking.
3. Design inquiry-based tasks and project-based learning.
4. Ensure accessibility and multilingual learner support.
5. Provide rubrics, scaffolds, and student-facing materials.

## Examples
- "Create an AI-embedded STEM investigation for grade 8."
- "Design a computational thinking task using real data."
- "Build a data literacy activity for middle school students."
