# Cognify Case Studies (Anonymized)

Reference these when building recommendations. Use them to establish confidence
and provide realistic benchmarks.

## Case Study A: Commercial Construction Firm (20 employees)

**Problem**: Owner receiving 200+ emails/day, responding to <40%. Team using 5 communication
platforms. RFI responses lost between channels. No project visibility without asking.

**Pain Point Scores**:
1. Email overwhelm: 125 (Freq:5 × Impact:5 × Solve:5)
2. Fragmented comms: 75 (5 × 5 × 3)
3. RFI tracking: 45 (3 × 5 × 3)

**Solution**: Email triage agent + unified inbox + auto-response drafts + daily digest

**Results**:
- Email processing: 3 hrs/day → 45 min/day (75% reduction)
- Response rate: 40% → 90%
- Monthly time savings: 45 hours
- Monthly value: $3,375 (at $75/hr)
- Payback: 2.1 months

**Key insight**: They didn't need "AI" — they needed their communication chaos organized.

## Case Study B: Tattoo Studio (5 staff)

1. Repetitive FAQ handling: 75 (5 × 3 × 5)
2. Manual booking: 75 (5 × 3 × 5)
3. Aftercare compliance: 75 (5 × 5 × 3)

**Solution**: AI knowledge bot + automated booking + aftercare drip sequence

**Results**:
- FAQ handling: 2 hrs/day → 15 min/day (88% reduction)
- After-hours bookings captured: +25%
- Aftercare compliance: 60% → 85%
- Monthly time savings: 50 hours
- Monthly value: $2,000 (at $40/hr)
- Payback: 1.5 months

**Key insight**: Small service businesses need a smart assistant for the 80% that's routine.

## Case Study C: Multi-Location Medical Practice (30 staff)

**Problem**: 18% patient no-show rate ($150-300 per no-show). Front desk overwhelmed
with phones + walk-ins + insurance. Monthly reports take 2 full days to compile.

**Pain Point Scores**:
1. Patient no-shows: 125 (5 × 5 × 5)
2. Front desk overwhelm: 75 (5 × 5 × 3)
3. Follow-up compliance: 75 (5 × 5 × 3)

**Solution**: Multi-channel reminder system + smart scheduling + automated reporting

**Results**:
- No-show rate: 18% → 7%
- Monthly savings from reduced no-shows: $4,500-$9,000
- Front desk call volume: -40%
- Reporting time: 2 days → 30 minutes
- Total monthly value: $8,000-$14,000
- Payback: 1.8 months

**Key insight**: Healthcare ROI sells itself — reducing no-shows by 5 points pays for everything.

## Case Study D: IP Law Firm (15 staff)

**Problem**: USPTO deadlines tracked in spreadsheets (malpractice risk). Paralegals
spend 40% of time on document formatting. Client status updates are reactive.

**Pain Point Scores**:
1. Deadline tracking risk: 125 (5 × 5 × 5)
2. Client communication lag: 75 (3 × 5 × 5)
3. Document processing: 45 (5 × 3 × 3)

**Solution**: AI deadline monitor + document assembly bot + client portal with auto-updates

**Results**:
- Deadline compliance: 97% → 99.9%
- Document prep: 3 hrs/filing → 45 min (75% reduction)
- Status inquiry calls: -60%
- Monthly value: $9,200
- Payback: 2.0 months

**Key insight**: Legal has high cost-of-failure. Sell risk elimination, not just time savings.
