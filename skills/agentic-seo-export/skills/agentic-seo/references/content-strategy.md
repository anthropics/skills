## Content Planning Workflow

### Step 1: Topic Discovery
1. **Topic Finder** → enter seed topic
2. Explore the visual topic map (bubble chart)
3. Sort by: Efficiency (volume / difficulty ratio)
4. Star topics to add to your content plan

### Step 2: Keyword Mapping
1. Take topic ideas → run through **Keyword Magic Tool**
2. Group keywords by topic cluster
3. **Keyword Strategy Builder** → auto-creates pillar + cluster structure

### Step 3: Content Briefs
**SEO Brief Generator** creates:
- Target keyword + supporting keywords
- Recommended word count (based on top 10)
- Suggested H2/H3 headings
- Questions to answer (from PAA boxes)
- Internal linking opportunities
- Competitor pages to beat

### Step 4: Writing
**Option A — AI-assisted writing:**
- AI Article Generator → get full draft in one click
- Edit for brand voice, accuracy, unique insights
- Use SEO Writing Assistant for real-time optimization

**Option B — Human writing with AI help:**
- Use brief from Step 3
- Write in your CMS or Google Docs with SEO Writing Assistant Chrome extension
- Score updates in real-time as you write

### Step 5: Optimization
**AI Search Optimizer** scans for:
- Missing semantic keywords
- Content structure issues
- Thin sections
- AI-specific ranking signals
- Readability/tone issues

Apply fixes from the guided checklist.

### Step 6: Publish & Distribute
1. Publish to WordPress (direct integration)
2. **Converter Tool** → generate social posts for each platform
3. **Converter Tool** → generate email newsletter version
4. Schedule social posts via Social Poster
5. Send email via Mailchimp integration

## Content Types & Best Practices

### Pillar Pages (Comprehensive Guides)
- 3,000–7,000 words
- Target head keyword (high volume)
- Links out to all cluster content
- Updated quarterly

### Cluster Content (Supporting Articles)
- 1,000–2,500 words
- Target long-tail keyword
- Links back to pillar
- Created monthly

### Product/Service Pages
- Target transactional keywords
- Focus: benefits, features, social proof, CTA
- Schema markup (Product, Service)
- Optimized for conversions

### Blog Posts
- Informational + commercial intent mix
- Internal links to product/service pages
- Updated when rankings drop or info goes stale

## Brand Voice Setup

In **AI Article Generator**:
1. Provide writing sample (500+ words of your content)
2. Tool analyzes: tone, vocabulary, sentence structure
3. Saves as brand voice profile
4. All future AI content matches your style

For agencies: Create separate brand voices per client

## Content Refresh Strategy

Signs a post needs refreshing:
- Rankings dropped >5 positions in 90 days
- Traffic down >20% year-over-year
- Information is outdated
- Competitors have overtaken it

**How to refresh** (using Semrush):
1. Run **On Page SEO Checker** on the URL
2. Check **AI Search Optimizer** for new AI-era requirements
3. Add missing keywords, update stats, expand thin sections
4. Re-optimize meta title/description
5. Add new internal links to recently published content
