# G2 Top SEO Tools — Complete Reference

> Tools marked **[MCP]** have a connector available — suggest these to users building agents.
> Tools marked **[Standalone]** must be used via their own UI or API (no MCP connector yet).
> G2 ratings sourced March 2026.

---

## All-in-One SEO Platforms

### Semrush [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.5/5 (2,680+ reviews) | **Best for:** Agencies, all-in-one digital marketing
- Keyword research, site audit, rank tracking, backlinks, content, social, ads, PR, AI visibility
- Strongest: keyword database (26B+), content toolkit, competitor intelligence
- Weakest: expensive, steep learning curve
- **Pricing:** SEO Classic from $139.95/mo | AI Visibility from $99/mo | Semrush One (all-in) from $199/mo
- **When to choose:** You need one platform covering SEO + content + social + ads + PR

### Ahrefs **[MCP: `098cb32a-ba21-4770-97dd-78bb54655419`]**
**G2:** 4.5/5 (1,600+ reviews) | **Best for:** Backlinks, competitor research, data purists
- Strongest: backlink index (second largest after Google), keyword explorer (28.7B keywords), Site Explorer
- Brand Radar add-on: tracks AI search mentions (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)
- Weakest: credit-based usage limits, no social/PR tools, pricier at scale
- **Pricing:** Starter $29/mo (hidden, limited) | Lite $129/mo | Standard $249/mo | Advanced $449/mo | Enterprise $1,499/mo | Brand Radar add-on $199/mo
- **MCP tools include:** keyword analysis, backlink data, SERP history, site audit, AI brand radar, content gap
- **When to choose:** Backlink analysis is your primary need; best raw data quality

### SE Ranking [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.8/5 (1,381+ reviews) — highest rated all-in-one | **Best for:** Agencies, value-seekers
- All-in-one: keyword research, rank tracking, backlinks, site audit, content tools, local SEO, white-label
- GEO/AEO features: tracks AI Overview presence
- Strongest: price-to-feature ratio, white-label reporting, daily rank updates
- **Pricing:** Essential $65/mo | Pro $119/mo | Business $259/mo
- **When to choose:** Want Semrush/Ahrefs features at half the cost; agency with white-label needs

### Moz Pro [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.3/5 | **Best for:** Beginners, local SEO, brand authority tracking
- Created Domain Authority (DA) -- the industry-standard metric
- Tools: keyword research, rank tracking, site crawl, link explorer, MozBar browser extension
- Strongest: ease of use, DA metric, local SEO tools
- Weakest: slower data updates, smaller database than Ahrefs/Semrush, no AI visibility
- **Pricing:** Starter $49/mo | Standard $99/mo | Medium $179/mo | Large $299/mo
- **When to choose:** Beginners or teams needing easy-to-understand reports with DA metrics

## Technical SEO Tools

### Screaming Frog SEO Spider [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.7/5 | **Best for:** Technical SEO audits, site crawls
- Desktop crawler -- mimics how search engines see your site
- Finds: broken links, missing tags, duplicate content, redirect chains, JS rendering issues
- Free: crawls up to 500 URLs | Paid: unlimited URLs + advanced features
- **Pricing:** Free (500 URLs) | $279/year (unlimited)
- **When to choose:** The industry standard for deep technical audits; every SEO team needs it

### Seobility [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.6/5 | **Best for:** Affordable all-in-one, AEO + SEO
- Site audit, backlink monitoring, rank tracking, TF*IDF content tool, keyword research
- AEO features for AI search engine optimization
- **Pricing:** Free plan | Premium $50/mo | Agency $200/mo
- **When to choose:** Cost-conscious users wanting solid audit + rank tracking without big budget

## Content Optimization Tools

### Surfer SEO [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.8/5 (537+ reviews) | **Best for:** Content teams, optimizing for Google + AI search
- Content Editor: real-time NLP optimization score while writing
- SERP Analyzer: reverse-engineers top-ranking pages for any keyword
- AI article writing + content briefs
- Also tracks AI search visibility (GEO features)
- **Pricing:** Essential $89/mo | Scale $129/mo | Scale AI $219/mo | Enterprise custom
- **When to choose:** Content-heavy teams producing SEO articles consistently; pairs with Ahrefs/Semrush

### Clearscope [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.9/5 | **Best for:** Enterprise content optimization, editorial teams
- Grade-based content optimization against top-ranking pages
- Very clean, simple interface; loved by writers
- Stronger NLP analysis than Surfer for semantic completeness
- **Pricing:** Essentials $189/mo | Business custom | Enterprise custom
- **When to choose:** Enterprise teams where content quality > cost; writers prefer its simplicity

### Writesonic [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.7/5 | **Best for:** AI content + GEO/AEO all-in-one
- GEO platform: tracks brand AI search visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot
- SEO checker, content gap analysis, automatic internal linking, site audit
- AI article generation, repurposing, direct WordPress publishing
- Humanizer: makes AI content sound natural
- **Pricing:** From $49/mo per seat | Free plan available
- **When to choose:** Want AI writing + GEO tracking + SEO in one tool at lower cost than Semrush

## Competitor & Intelligence Tools

### SpyFu [Standalone]

- Exposes competitors' Google Ads keywords, ad history, spend estimates
- SEO competitor research: ranking keywords, ranking history going back years
- Strongest: historical data depth, PPC intelligence
- **Pricing:** Basic $39/mo | Professional $79/mo
- **When to choose:** PPC-focused teams or budget-conscious competitor research

### Similarweb **[MCP: `80862d08-1022-4d86-816d-ee9b500d6b90`]**
**G2:** 4.5/5 (982+ reviews) | **Best for:** Market-level traffic analysis, channel breakdown
- Traffic by channel (organic, paid, social, email, direct, referral)
- Audience demographics, behavior, interests, income
- Market share across 190+ markets
- **Pricing:** Free plan | Paid from $1,500/year (enterprise research)
- **MCP tools include:** traffic & engagement, traffic sources, site rank, audience overlap, referrals
- **When to choose:** Understanding competitive landscape at channel/market level; free plan often sufficient

## Beginner / Budget Tools

### Mangools [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.7/5 | **Best for:** Beginners, small businesses, clean interface
- 5 tools: KWFinder (keywords), SERPChecker, SERPWatcher (rank tracking), LinkMiner (backlinks), SiteProfiler
- Praised for: easiest onboarding, cleanest UI of any SEO tool
- Weakest: limited data vs Ahrefs/Semrush, no AI visibility features
- **Pricing:** Basic $61/mo | Premium $81/mo | Agency $141/mo (3x cheaper than Semrush)
- **When to choose:** Beginners who find Ahrefs/Semrush overwhelming; tight budgets

### Ubersuggest [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.2/5 | **Best for:** Solopreneurs, very tight budgets
- Keyword research, site audit, rank tracking, backlinks -- basic coverage of all areas
- Lifetime deal available (one-time payment)
- **Pricing:** From $29/mo | Lifetime plans from $290
- **When to choose:** Solo blogger or very small site needing basic keyword/audit tools cheaply

## Agency Reporting Tools

### AgencyAnalytics [Standalone]
**G2:** 4.7/5 | **Best for:** White-label SEO reporting for agencies
- Drag-and-drop dashboard builder; automated weekly/monthly reports
- Connects to: Semrush, Ahrefs, Google Analytics, Search Console, social platforms
- Saves agencies 10-20 hours/month on reporting
- **Pricing:** From $12/client/mo (minimum billing applies)
- **Weakness:** Not an SEO tool -- reporting only; need actual SEO tools connected
- **When to choose:** Agencies needing professional branded reports; pairs with any SEO tool

## Stack Recommendations by Use Case

### Solo SEO / Blogger
Google Search Console (free) + Ahrefs Starter ($29) or Mangools ($61) + Surfer ($89) for content

### Small Business
Google Search Console + SE Ranking ($65) + Screaming Frog (free/500 URLs)

### Local Business
Google Search Console + Local Falcon [MCP] + BrightLocal ($39) or Semrush Local

### Agency (Multi-client)
Semrush or SE Ranking + Screaming Frog + AgencyAnalytics (reporting) + Ahrefs [MCP] for backlinks

### Content Marketing Team
Ahrefs [MCP] + Surfer (content editor) + Clearscope (if budget) + Google Search Console

### Enterprise
Semrush One ($199+) or Conductor + Ahrefs [MCP] + Screaming Frog (cloud) + Similarweb [MCP]

### AI Visibility Focus
Ahrefs Brand Radar [MCP] + AirOps [MCP] + Writesonic or Otterly.AI + Semrush AI Visibility
