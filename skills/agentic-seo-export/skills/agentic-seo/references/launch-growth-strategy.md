# Launch Strategy & Growth Channels

## Product / Feature Launch Framework (`launch-strategy`)

### Pre-Launch (4–6 weeks before)
**Build the list:**
- "Notify me" landing page → collect emails
- Waitlist with referral incentive (move up the queue by sharing)
- Seed Product Hunt with followers before launch day
- Prime relevant communities (Reddit, Slack groups, Discord)

**Content preparation:**
- Demo video (60–90 seconds, shows core value immediately)
- Launch blog post (story + product benefits)
- Social announcement graphics
- Email sequence (3-5 emails: teaser → launch → follow-up)
- Press kit (logo, screenshots, founder bio, one-liner)

**Outreach list:**
- Journalists who cover your space (use AI PR Toolkit)
- Newsletters in your niche
- Influencers / creators your audience follows
- Partner integrations for cross-promotion

### Launch Day
**Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9am PT (maximizes US + EU overlap)

**Launch sequence (same day):**
1. 6am: Send to waitlist/email list
2. 8am: Product Hunt goes live → share with supporters for upvotes
3. 9am: Post on Twitter/LinkedIn
4. 10am: Post in communities (Slack, Discord, Reddit) where relevant
5. 11am: Outreach to journalists with embargo lifted
6. All day: Respond to every comment/question

**Metrics to track:**
- Email open rate + clicks
- Signups (hourly)
- Product Hunt ranking (goal: top 5 of the day)
- Social shares + mentions
- Press coverage

### Post-Launch (1–4 weeks after)
- Retarget visitors who didn't convert
- Follow up with journalists who didn't respond
- Write "launch lessons" post (transparency content performs well)
- Analyze: which channel drove highest quality signups?
- Iterate: what did users immediately ask for/complain about?

## Referral Program Design (`referral-program`)

### Mechanics
**Double-sided rewards work best:**
- Referrer gets: X months free / account credits / cash
- Referred gets: discount / extended trial / bonus feature

**Trigger timing:**
- After activation (user has seen value)
- After first success milestone ("You just hit 1,000 visitors!")
- After positive support interaction

### Copy
- Headline: lead with what the friend gets ("Give your friend X")
- Social proof: "X people have already referred a friend"
- Easy sharing: pre-written copy + direct link + social share buttons

### Tracking
- Unique referral links per user
- Attribution window: 30–90 days
- Track: shares → clicks → signups → activations → revenue

## Free Tool SEO Strategy (`free-tool-strategy`)

### Tool Ideas by Category

**SaaS marketing:**
- ROI calculator for your product
- Score/grade tool ("Website SEO Score")
- Generator ("Meta Description Generator")
- Checker ("Link Checker", "Broken Image Checker")

**E-commerce:**
- Product margin calculator
- Shipping cost estimator
- Supplier comparison tool

**Local business:**
- Local SEO audit tool
- Review response generator
- Business hours formatter

### SEO Value of Free Tools
- Attract backlinks: tools get linked to by blogs and resources pages
- Target high-intent keywords: "free [tool type]" searches
- Establish topical authority in your niche
- Drive warm leads (people using your free tool are your ideal customers)

### Tool Launch Checklist
- [ ] Name includes the keyword: "Free Meta Description Generator"
- [ ] Schema: `SoftwareApplication` on the page
- [ ] Email gate for export/full results
- [ ] "Built with [Your Product]" attribution
- [ ] Related blog content linking to/from tool
- [ ] List on: free tools directories, Product Hunt, BetaList

## Pricing Strategy (`pricing-strategy`)

### Models
| Model | Best For | Risk |
|-------|---------|------|
| Per seat | Teams, SaaS | Friction with growth |
| Usage-based | APIs, infra | Unpredictable revenue |
| Tiered flat | Most SaaS | Value metric mismatch |
| Freemium | Viral/PLG | High free-to-paid ratio needed |
| Annual prepay | Cash flow | Higher churn barrier |

### Anchoring Your Tiers
- **Starter**: removes biggest pain point, limits scale
- **Pro/Growth**: full value for core customer (this is your real product)
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing, removes all limits, SLA

**Price psychology:**
- $49 vs $50: charm pricing has diminishing effect at higher prices
- $97/mo vs $99/mo: perceived as meaningfully cheaper
- Annual discount: 15–20% is standard, below 10% doesn't change behavior
- Show monthly cost for annual: "$X/month billed annually" converts better

### When to Raise Prices
- NPS is high but revenue is low → underpriced
- Sales cycle is short + no negotiation → underpriced
- New features launched without pricing change
- Acquisition cost risen but price hasn't

## Social Content Strategy (`social-content`)

### Platform-Specific Best Practices

**LinkedIn:**
- Hook: Bold first line that stops the scroll (no "I'm excited to announce")
- Format: Short paragraphs, 1-2 lines each (never walls of text)
- High performers: behind-the-scenes, contrarian takes, data reveals
- Optimal length: 150–300 words
- Best times: Tue–Thu, 8–10am

**Twitter/X:**
- Thread > single tweet for complex ideas
- Hook tweet: one clear, interesting statement
- End thread: CTA or question to drive replies
- Images/charts: 2× the engagement of text-only

**Instagram:**
- Carousel: highest reach format currently
- Slide 1: hook/problem
- Slides 2–8: value
- Slide 9–10: CTA
- Caption: front-load value before "more"

**TikTok/Reels:**
- Hook within first 2 seconds (or swipe)
- Pattern interrupt: start in middle of action
- Duration: 15–30 seconds for algorithm, 60–90 for educational
- Text overlay: 85%+ watch without sound

### Content Calendar Balance (Rule of Thirds)
- ⅓ Educational / value: tips, how-tos, data
- ⅓ Brand / culture: team, process, behind-scenes
- ⅓ Promotional: product, offers, case studies

## Marketing Ideas Generation (`marketing-ideas`)

### High-ROI Channels by Stage

**Early stage (0–1k customers):**
- Direct outreach (cold email, LinkedIn DMs)
- Communities (Reddit, Slack, Discord, Facebook Groups)
- Content SEO (long-tail, low competition)
- Partner integrations + co-marketing
- Founder-led social (personal brand)

**Growth stage (1k–10k customers):**
- PPC (Google Ads — branded + competitor)
- Affiliate/referral program
- Content at scale (programmatic + AI)
- Email nurture sequences
- Webinars + events

**Scale stage (10k+ customers):**
- PR + analyst relations
- Paid social (Meta, LinkedIn)
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Product-led growth loops
- Enterprise sales + ABM

### Quick Win Marketing Tactics
1. **Competitor targeting**: bid on competitor brand keywords in Google Ads
2. **Review campaigns**: ask happy customers for G2/Capterra reviews (free traffic)
3. **Content refresh**: update top 10 pages from 2 years ago (quick ranking recovery)
4. **Newsletter sponsorships**: find niche newsletters with your ICP audience
5. **LinkedIn articles from execs**: repurpose blog posts as thought leadership
6. **Reddit AMAs**: build authority in relevant subreddits
7. **Partner content swaps**: write for each other's blogs (backlinks + audience)
