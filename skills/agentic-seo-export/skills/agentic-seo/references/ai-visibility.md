# AI Visibility — Optimization Playbook

## Why AI Visibility Matters

Traditional SEO = rank on Google's blue links
AI SEO = get cited/recommended by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, etc.

**Key difference:** AI tools don't show a list — they give ONE answer. If that answer isn't you, you're invisible.

---

## How AI Decides Who to Cite

AI models trust sources that are:
1. **Authoritative** — high-trust domains, established expertise
2. **Cited in media** — mentioned in reputable outlets
3. **Well-structured** — clear facts, headings, FAQ format
4. **Frequently referenced** — cited by other trusted sources
5. **Semantically complete** — answers the full question, not just part of it

## AI Visibility Workflow (Semrush)

### Phase 1: Audit Your Current Position
1. **Visibility Overview**: Check your AI share of voice score
2. **Brand Performance**: Read sentiment (positive/neutral/negative mentions in AI answers)
3. **Prompt Tracking**: Add 10–20 key prompts your customers might ask
   - Example: "best [your category] software for [use case]"
   - Example: "how do I [solve problem your product solves]"

### Phase 2: Competitive Intelligence
1. **Competitor Research**: Enter top 3 rivals
2. Find prompts where they're mentioned but you aren't
3. Analyze their cited **sources** — what media/content gets them cited?
4. Note their **sentiment patterns** — what narrative do AI tools have about them?

### Phase 3: Content Gaps
1. **Prompt Research**: Browse actual user prompts in your industry
2. Identify: what questions are being asked that you don't have content for?
3. **Narrative Drivers**: What topics/claims drive AI mentions for leaders in your space?

### Phase 4: Close the Gap

#### Content Actions
- Create FAQ pages targeting exact prompt formats
- Write comprehensive "what is X" and "how to X" guides
- Ensure content answers the full question (not just keyword-stuffed)
- Add structured data (Schema.org) — especially FAQ, HowTo, Article schemas

#### Technical Actions (via Site Audit)
- Ensure AI crawlers can access your site (check robots.txt)
- Fix: slow load times, redirect chains, blocked resources
- Add clear canonical tags
- Improve E-E-A-T signals (author bios, citations, about page)

#### Authority Actions (via AI PR)
- Get featured in AI-cited media outlets (find them in AI-Cited Media tool)
- Build backlinks from authoritative sources AI tools trust
- Create Wikipedia-worthy citations for your brand claims

### Phase 5: Monitor & Iterate
- Track daily prompt rankings in **Prompt Tracking**
- Monitor **sentiment shifts** after publishing new content
- Set up **Growth Actions** to get weekly prioritized recommendations

## E-E-A-T for AI Visibility

Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) directly maps to AI trust signals:

| Signal | How to Demonstrate |
|--------|-------------------|
| Experience | Case studies, real results, customer stories |
| Expertise | Author credentials, certifications, detailed guides |
| Authoritativeness | Mentions in major publications, industry awards |
| Trustworthiness | Accurate info, citations, transparent about-us, reviews |

---

## Content Formats AI Loves

1. **Direct answer format**: Lead with the answer, then explain
2. **Lists**: Numbered/bulleted answers are frequently pulled
3. **Definitions**: "X is defined as..." structures
4. **Comparison tables**: Highly citable for "vs" queries
5. **Statistics with sources**: AI loves citing specific data points
6. **Step-by-step guides**: For "how to" prompts
