## Evaluation Dimensions

### 1. Structure
- Did the candidate follow the framework? (PS: 5 steps, AT: 5 steps)
- Were transitions between sections clearly signaled (waypointing)?
- Did they state a game plan upfront and check in with the interviewer?

### 2. User Empathy
- Was the chosen persona specific and vivid?
- Were pain points grounded in real user experience, not abstract?
- Did the candidate demonstrate genuine understanding of user needs?

### 3. Strategic Thinking
- Did choices connect to business outcomes?
- Was prioritization explicitly justified (not just asserted)?
- Did the candidate show awareness of Meta's ecosystem and competitive context?

### 4. Metrics Quality (AT focus)
- Was the NSM defensible and well-justified vs. alternatives?
- Were guardrail metrics meaningful and non-obvious?
- Did goal-setting include a justified number, not just a direction?

### 5. Communication Speed
- Did the candidate communicate at a clear, confident pace?
- Did they avoid over-explaining or under-explaining?
- Did they use crisp language, or were there long rambling sections?

### 6. Outcome Focus
- Did the candidate stay in problem space before jumping to solutions?
- Were solutions tied back to the specific problem and user identified?
- Did the recommendation feel earned, not predetermined?
