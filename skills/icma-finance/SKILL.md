# ICMA Finance & Management Accounting Skill

## Purpose
Help students and professionals with ICMA exam preparation and
management accounting tasks including cost analysis, financial
ratios, budgeting, and professional finance concepts.

## Key Topics Covered
- Financial ratio analysis (liquidity, profitability, solvency)
- Cost accounting (job costing, process costing, activity-based costing)
- Budgeting and variance analysis
- Capital budgeting and investment appraisal
- Management accounting reports
- ICMA exam practice questions and explanations

## How to Use
When a user asks about any finance or accounting topic related to
ICMA, provide clear step-by-step explanations with worked examples,
formulas, and exam tips.

## Examples
- "Explain the current ratio and how to calculate it"
- "Help me prepare for ICMA cost accounting paper"
- "What is variance analysis in budgeting?"
- "Solve this NPV capital budgeting problem"
