# Summer Daze Pink

Playful pinks and teals for an upbeat summer vibe.

## Color Palette

- **Cream Soda**: `#F6E4D5` - Base / background
- **Aqua Mist**: `#C9E1E5` - Light neutral
- **Bubblegum**: `#EFBAD6` - Soft accent
- **Teal**: `#389998` - Mid-tone accent
- **Magenta Pop**: `#C55894` - Deep accent
- **Near Black**: `#271F22` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins (use Poppins Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Youthful consumer brands, events, social-first campaigns.

> Category: Bold & Fun  ·  Mode: light
