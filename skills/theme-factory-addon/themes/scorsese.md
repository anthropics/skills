# Scorsese

Operatic crimson and gold over black — the saturated heat of Goodfellas and Mean Streets.

## Color Palette

- **Black**: `#120A0A` - Base / background
- **Oxblood**: `#4A0E10` - Surface
- **Crimson**: `#A11217` - Muted accent
- **Scarlet**: `#D6322B` - Accent
- **Gold**: `#C8972E` - Bright accent
- **Warm Ivory**: `#E8D6B8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Bold dramatic brands, nightlife and culture, high-impact storytelling.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: dark
