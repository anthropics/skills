# Nike

Just black and white with the unmistakable Volt accent.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Volt**: `#CEFF00` - Light accent
- **Cool Gray**: `#8D8D8D` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#111111` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Sportswear and fitness, bold athletic campaigns, high-contrast decks.
