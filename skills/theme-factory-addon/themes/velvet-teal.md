# Velvet Teal

Deep teal and near-black with a cool pearl lift.

## Color Palette

- **Ivory**: `#F2EFEA` - Base / background
- **Pearl**: `#DFE5F3` - Light neutral
- **Teal**: `#557373` - Accent
- **Bitter Olive**: `#272401` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#0D0D0D` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Modern luxury brands, premium hospitality, sleek editorial layouts.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
