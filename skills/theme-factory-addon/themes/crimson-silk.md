# Crimson Silk

Soft blush rising to deep lacquer red.

## Color Palette

- **Shell**: `#F3ECE4` - Base / background
- **Rosewood**: `#D9B6A3` - Light neutral
- **Coral Clay**: `#D88B79` - Accent
- **Lacquer Red**: `#A71F13` - Deep accent
- **Oxblood**: `#340A10` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Premium beauty, fashion houses, bold romantic luxury decks.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
