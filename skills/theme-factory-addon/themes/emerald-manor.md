# Emerald Manor

Stately green and gold with a soft cream lift.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#F4F4F4` - Base / background
- **Cream**: `#EDDBCD` - Light neutral
- **Antique Gold**: `#C0B283` - Accent
- **Forest**: `#404A42` - Deep accent
- **Midnight**: `#192231` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Heritage luxury, law and finance, refined corporate identity.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
