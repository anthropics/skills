# Sandy Neutrals

Warm sand and rosy clay neutrals.

## Color Palette

- **Shell**: `#F5E8E0` - Base / background
- **Rose Mist**: `#F5D4D1` - Light neutral
- **Peach Cream**: `#EED3C2` - Soft accent
- **Latte**: `#C5B4A3` - Mid-tone accent
- **Mushroom**: `#A78F7C` - Deep accent
- **Cocoa**: `#7F6152` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Marcellus (use Marcellus Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Mulish

## Best Used For

Skincare and beauty, artisanal products, soft brand systems.

> Category: Modern Boho & Neutrals  ·  Mode: light
