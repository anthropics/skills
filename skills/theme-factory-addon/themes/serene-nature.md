# Serene Nature

Soft botanicals and watery blues for a calm, natural feel.

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#FDF1DB` - Base / background
- **Oat**: `#DFD5C6` - Light neutral
- **Seaglass**: `#A6CBD3` - Soft accent
- **Fern**: `#96AB88` - Mid-tone accent
- **Lake Blue**: `#4F8FA8` - Deep accent
- **Pine**: `#597B60` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Fraunces (use Fraunces Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Karla

## Best Used For

Spa and wellness, eco products, gentle lifestyle content.
