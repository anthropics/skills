# Spaceduck

A spacefaring palette — inky cosmos with teal, lavender, and cream.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#0F111B` - Base / background
- **Teal**: `#00A3CC` - Surface
- **Gold**: `#E39400` - Muted accent
- **Lavender**: `#B3A1E6` - Accent
- **Green**: `#5CCC96` - Bright accent
- **Cream**: `#ECF0C1` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Mono (use Space Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Sci-fi and space brands, futuristic dashboards, moody tech decks.

> Category: Neon & Retro  ·  Mode: dark
