# Cobalt2

Wes Bos's signature theme — deep blue with electric yellow and orange.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#193549` - Base / background
- **Blue**: `#0088FF` - Surface
- **Orange**: `#FF9D00` - Muted accent
- **Green**: `#3AD900` - Accent
- **Yellow**: `#FFC600` - Bright accent
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Mono (use Space Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Bold developer brands, course and tutorial content, punchy tech talks.

> Category: Neon & Retro  ·  Mode: dark
