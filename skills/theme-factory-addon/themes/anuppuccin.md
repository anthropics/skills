# AnuPpuccin

A Catppuccin-based theme loaded with rainbow accents over a pastel night.

## Color Palette

- **Base**: `#1E1E2E` - Base / background
- **Blue**: `#89B4FA` - Surface
- **Mauve**: `#CBA6F7` - Muted accent
- **Peach**: `#FAB387` - Accent
- **Green**: `#A6E3A1` - Bright accent
- **Text**: `#CDD6F4` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: IBM Plex Mono (use IBM Plex Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: IBM Plex Sans

## Best Used For

Colorful note systems, expressive dashboards, playful dark UIs.

> Category: Obsidian & Calm  ·  Mode: dark
