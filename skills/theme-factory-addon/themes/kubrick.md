# Kubrick

Stark, symmetrical dread — Overlook cream and burnt orange cut by carpet red (The Shining).

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#E8E0CC` - Base / background
- **Bone**: `#D8CBA8` - Light neutral
- **Burnt Orange**: `#C8642A` - Soft accent
- **Brown**: `#6E4A2A` - Mid-tone accent
- **Carpet Red**: `#B01F2E` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#1A1410` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Bold retro brands, design-led decks, striking editorial layouts.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: light
