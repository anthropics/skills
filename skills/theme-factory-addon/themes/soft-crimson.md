# Soft Crimson

Tender creams deepening into berry and plum.

## Color Palette

- **Shell**: `#F5E8E0` - Base / background
- **Linen**: `#EEE8DC` - Light neutral
- **Rose Quartz**: `#F8DEDC` - Soft accent
- **Dusty Rose**: `#DAA2A1` - Mid-tone accent
- **Wine Rose**: `#914955` - Deep accent
- **Plum**: `#683147` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Nunito Sans

## Best Used For

Beauty and bridal, boutique brands, romantic editorial decks.

> Category: Feminine & Soft  ·  Mode: light
