# Storaro

Bold color-theory saturation — amber, crimson, and deep cyan (Apocalypse Now, The Conformist).

## Color Palette

- **Jungle Black**: `#10160F` - Base / background
- **Deep Cyan**: `#14484E` - Surface
- **Crimson**: `#C0281E` - Muted accent
- **Teal**: `#2E7A78` - Accent
- **Amber**: `#E08A2A` - Bright accent
- **Gold**: `#E8B84A` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Expressive brands, art-house aesthetics, vivid symbolic color decks.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: dark
