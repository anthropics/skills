# Vermeer

The quiet light of Girl with a Pearl Earring — ultramarine and ochre in shadow.

## Color Palette

- **Umber Ground**: `#2A2018` - Base / background
- **Ultramarine**: `#1F4E8C` - Surface
- **Ochre Turban**: `#C8912E` - Accent
- **Warm Flesh**: `#D8A985` - Bright accent
- **Ivory**: `#EAD9B8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Refined editorial, premium brands, contemplative fine-art layouts.
