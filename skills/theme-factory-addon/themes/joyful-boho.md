# Joyful Boho

Bright boho mix of coral, sky, and deep teal.

## Color Palette

- **Apricot**: `#F8D4BB` - Base / background
- **Peach**: `#FAC8C0` - Light neutral
- **Sky**: `#B2D7E8` - Soft accent
- **Cornflower**: `#5F8BC1` - Mid-tone accent
- **Coral**: `#D56F53` - Deep accent
- **Deep Teal**: `#014552` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins (use Poppins Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Creative studios, festivals, lively product and travel content.
