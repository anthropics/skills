# Deakins

Orange-and-teal mastery — burnt ember against deep teal shadow (Blade Runner 2049, Sicario).

## Color Palette

- **Black**: `#0E0F12` - Base / background
- **Teal Shadow**: `#163A3E` - Surface
- **Teal**: `#2E6E72` - Muted accent
- **Ember Orange**: `#D8662A` - Accent
- **Amber Haze**: `#E89B3C` - Bright accent
- **Dust**: `#E8C99A` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Cinematic brand films, dramatic product launches, atmospheric decks.
