# Minimal

The Obsidian favorite — neutral grays with one calm configurable accent.

## Color Palette

- **Paper**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Light Gray**: `#F5F6F8` - Light neutral
- **Border Gray**: `#D5D8DC` - Soft accent
- **Muted**: `#9AA0A6` - Mid-tone accent
- **Accent Blue**: `#4C8BF5` - Deep accent
- **Text**: `#2E3338` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: IBM Plex Mono (use IBM Plex Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: IBM Plex Sans

## Best Used For

Distraction-free writing, clean knowledge bases, restrained light UIs.

> Category: Obsidian & Calm  ·  Mode: light
