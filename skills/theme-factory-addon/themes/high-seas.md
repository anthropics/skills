# High Seas

Deep ocean navy lifted by bright sea spray.

## Color Palette

- **Sea Foam White**: `#EDEDEE` - Base / background
- **Cloud Blue**: `#D6E0EA` - Light neutral
- **Tide**: `#72A6B7` - Soft accent
- **Harbor Gray**: `#898989` - Mid-tone accent
- **Deep Marine**: `#18617F` - Deep accent
- **Midnight Navy**: `#092846` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Quicksand (use Quicksand Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Open Sans

## Best Used For

Maritime and travel brands, finance, trust-building corporate decks.

> Category: Coastal & Calm  ·  Mode: light
