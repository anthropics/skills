# Spotify

Spotify's vivid green against deep black.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Bright Green**: `#1ED760` - Light accent
- **Spotify Green**: `#1DB954` - Deep accent
- **Spotify Black**: `#191414` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Music and audio, youthful tech products, energetic marketing.
