# Wong Kar-wai

Lush, smoky saturation — deep red and emerald with gold (In the Mood for Love).

## Color Palette

- **Noir Black**: `#161010` - Base / background
- **Deep Red**: `#6E121A` - Surface
- **Crimson**: `#A81C24` - Muted accent
- **Emerald**: `#1E6E54` - Accent
- **Gold**: `#C8972E` - Bright accent
- **Warm Cream**: `#E8D2A8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Romantic and luxury brands, moody fashion, richly saturated editorial.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: dark
