# Google

Google's four primaries: blue, red, yellow, and green.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Google Yellow**: `#F4B400` - Light neutral
- **Google Blue**: `#4285F4` - Accent
- **Google Green**: `#0F9D58` - Deep accent
- **Google Red**: `#DB4437` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Consumer tech, friendly product UIs, approachable corporate decks.

> Category: Iconic Brands  ·  Mode: light
