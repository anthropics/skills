# Matisse

Fauvist joy from The Dance — flat, fearless terracotta, cobalt, and green.

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#EFE6D2` - Base / background
- **Flesh**: `#D98C5A` - Light neutral
- **Pink**: `#E0728C` - Soft accent
- **Kelly Green**: `#3A8C4E` - Mid-tone accent
- **Cobalt**: `#2E6BB0` - Deep accent
- **Terracotta**: `#C8472C` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Playful creative brands, bold campaigns, vibrant cut-out style layouts.
