# Lubezki

Natural light at the edge of cold — snowy blue-gray lit by a single ember (The Revenant).

## Color Palette

- **Cold Slate**: `#1E262B` - Base / background
- **Blue Gray**: `#3A4A52` - Surface
- **Ember**: `#B5662E` - Muted accent
- **Frost**: `#6E828A` - Accent
- **Pale Sky**: `#A8BAC0` - Bright accent
- **Bone**: `#D6DBD8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Naturalistic brand films, outdoor and wellness, atmospheric editorial.
