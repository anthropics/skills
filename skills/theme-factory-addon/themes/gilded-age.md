# Gilded Age

Champagne and gold over deep oxblood.

## Color Palette

- **Stone**: `#D8D0C5` - Base / background
- **Champagne**: `#D9C4A9` - Light neutral
- **Gold**: `#D9B061` - Accent
- **Bronze**: `#8D6F57` - Deep accent
- **Oxblood**: `#3F0D0C` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Luxury goods, jewelry, gala and event branding, opulent decks.
