# Mondrian

De Stijl in its purest form — primary red, blue, and yellow gridded by black.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#F4F3EE` - Base / background
- **Yellow**: `#F2C200` - Light neutral
- **Red**: `#D5212E` - Accent
- **Blue**: `#1E3A8A` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#14110E` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Graphic-forward brands, bold modernist decks, design-school aesthetics.
