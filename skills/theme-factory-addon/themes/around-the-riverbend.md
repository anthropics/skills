# Around the Riverbend

Riverstone neutrals with muted blue and sand.

## Color Palette

- **River Mist**: `#C5DAE6` - Base / background
- **Pebble**: `#BEBBC2` - Light neutral
- **Sand**: `#BBA48D` - Soft accent
- **Sage Gray**: `#9AA7A0` - Mid-tone accent
- **Bluestone**: `#8391A7` - Deep accent
- **Olive Gray**: `#767064` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Quicksand (use Quicksand Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Open Sans

## Best Used For

Quiet lifestyle brands, architecture and interiors, calm reports.

> Category: Coastal & Calm  ·  Mode: light
