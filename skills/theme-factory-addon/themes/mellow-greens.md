# Mellow Greens

Muted greens and warm clay for an understated organic palette.

## Color Palette

- **Mist Green**: `#D0DDD7` - Base / background
- **Petal Clay**: `#F2D2C2` - Light neutral
- **Eucalyptus**: `#A3BFB3` - Soft accent
- **Almond**: `#C6A894` - Mid-tone accent
- **Juniper**: `#648E7B` - Deep accent
- **Graphite**: `#6D6E71` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Fraunces (use Fraunces Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Karla

## Best Used For

Plant and garden brands, holistic services, soft editorial layouts.

> Category: Earthy & Grounded  ·  Mode: light
