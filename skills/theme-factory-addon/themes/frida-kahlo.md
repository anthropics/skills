# Frida Kahlo

Vivid Mexican folk color — cobalt, viridian, scarlet, and marigold in full bloom.

## Color Palette

- **Bone**: `#EDE3D0` - Base / background
- **Marigold**: `#E89B1C` - Light neutral
- **Viridian**: `#1F7A4D` - Soft accent
- **Cobalt**: `#2E5EAA` - Mid-tone accent
- **Magenta**: `#B5277A` - Deep accent
- **Scarlet**: `#C8262C` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Festive brands, cultural campaigns, bold expressive editorial.

> Category: Master Painters  ·  Mode: light
