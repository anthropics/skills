# Calm Sapphire

Cool sapphire blues for a composed, trustworthy mood.

## Color Palette

- **Powder Blue**: `#D6E0EA` - Base / background
- **Cornflower**: `#84A2C9` - Light neutral
- **Steel**: `#898993` - Soft accent
- **Denim**: `#445A79` - Mid-tone accent
- **Sapphire**: `#344E95` - Deep accent
- **Ink**: `#1C1D37` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Quicksand (use Quicksand Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Open Sans

## Best Used For

Professional services, SaaS, consulting, dependable corporate content.
