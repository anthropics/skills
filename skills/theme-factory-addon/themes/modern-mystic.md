# Modern Mystic

Romantic lilac and amber over deep aubergine.

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#F5EAE1` - Base / background
- **Pale Gray**: `#D9D8DD` - Light neutral
- **Lilac**: `#D7C2EE` - Soft accent
- **Amber**: `#E4AD75` - Mid-tone accent
- **Ochre**: `#C07A35` - Deep accent
- **Aubergine**: `#592E6B` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Playfair Display (use Playfair Display Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: EB Garamond

## Best Used For

Mystic and astrology brands, creative portfolios, romantic invitations.
