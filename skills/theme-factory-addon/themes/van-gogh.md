# Van Gogh

The swirling night of The Starry Night — cobalt and indigo lit by golden stars.

## Color Palette

- **Indigo Night**: `#0B1A3A` - Base / background
- **Cypress**: `#1A3A2A` - Surface
- **Cobalt**: `#1E4A8A` - Muted accent
- **Swirl Blue**: `#4A78B5` - Accent
- **Star Yellow**: `#F4C430` - Bright accent
- **Pale Moon**: `#E8E2B0` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Expressive brands, creative campaigns, vivid storytelling decks.
