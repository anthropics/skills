# Netflix

Netflix red and dark red over cinema black.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Dark Red**: `#B81D24` - Light accent
- **Netflix Red**: `#E50914` - Deep accent
- **Cinema Black**: `#221F1F` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Entertainment and streaming, dramatic launches, cinematic decks.
