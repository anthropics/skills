# Modern Pop

High-contrast pop palette with electric magenta and blue.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Turquoise**: `#47BFBE` - Light neutral
- **Gray**: `#898993` - Soft accent
- **Hot Pink**: `#E83698` - Mid-tone accent
- **Electric Blue**: `#1C5AE4` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#000000` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins (use Poppins Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Tech and entertainment launches, bold campaigns, attention-grabbing slides.

> Category: Bold & Fun  ·  Mode: light
