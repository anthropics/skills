# Sandy Shores

Warm shoreline sand against deep tidal blue.

## Color Palette

- **Beach Sand**: `#EFE6D6` - Base / background
- **Driftwood**: `#B8B8AC` - Light neutral
- **Toffee**: `#CBA378` - Soft accent
- **Clay**: `#C86D39` - Mid-tone accent
- **Deep Tide**: `#2E4C63` - Deep accent
- **Charcoal**: `#2C2927` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Quicksand (use Quicksand Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Open Sans

## Best Used For

Coastal hospitality, travel, warm-meets-cool lifestyle decks.

> Category: Coastal & Calm  ·  Mode: light
