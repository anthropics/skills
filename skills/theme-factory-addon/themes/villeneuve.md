# Villeneuve

Monumental desert minimalism — sand and spice ochre with cold slate (Dune).

## Color Palette

- **Bone**: `#F0E6CE` - Base / background
- **Sand**: `#E8D2A6` - Light neutral
- **Dune Ochre**: `#C89A5A` - Soft accent
- **Spice Amber**: `#C8762E` - Mid-tone accent
- **Slate Blue**: `#5A6E7A` - Deep accent
- **Charcoal**: `#2A2622` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Epic minimalist brands, sci-fi and architecture, monumental decks.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: light
