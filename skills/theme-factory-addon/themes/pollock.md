# Pollock

The drip energy of Autumn Rhythm — bone canvas tangled with black, rust, and teal.

## Color Palette

- **Bone**: `#F5F0E6` - Base / background
- **Canvas**: `#E8DFC8` - Light neutral
- **Rust**: `#A6522C` - Soft accent
- **Teal Splash**: `#2E6E6A` - Mid-tone accent
- **Umber**: `#6B4A2B` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#1A1714` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Creative studios, expressive portfolios, energetic abstract layouts.

> Category: Master Painters  ·  Mode: light
