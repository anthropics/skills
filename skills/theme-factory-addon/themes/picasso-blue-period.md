# Picasso Blue Period

Melancholy blues of The Old Guitarist — somber and soulful, lit by a single ochre.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Blue**: `#0B2A4A` - Base / background
- **Teal-Blue**: `#14506E` - Surface
- **Steel**: `#2E7A99` - Accent
- **Muted Ochre**: `#C7A26B` - Bright accent
- **Blue-Gray**: `#A8B6BE` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Reflective brand stories, fine-art decks, moody editorial design.
