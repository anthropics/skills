# Airbnb

Airbnb's Rausch coral with Babu teal and warm neutrals.

## Color Palette

- **Babu Teal**: `#00A699` - Base / background
- **Arches**: `#FC642D` - Light neutral
- **Rausch**: `#FF5A5F` - Accent
- **Foggy**: `#767676` - Deep accent
- **Hof**: `#484848` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Travel and hospitality, community marketplaces, warm lifestyle brands.
