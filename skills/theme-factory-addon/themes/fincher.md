# Fincher

Sickly desaturated green-cyan and sodium amber in deep shadow (Se7en, Fight Club).

## Color Palette

- **Black Green**: `#0E1310` - Base / background
- **Dark Slate**: `#1C2622` - Surface
- **Murk Green**: `#3E4A38` - Muted accent
- **Cyan Steel**: `#4A6660` - Accent
- **Sodium Amber**: `#9A7B3A` - Bright accent
- **Ash**: `#B8BEB4` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Tech-noir brands, moody product UIs, tense cinematic storytelling.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: dark
