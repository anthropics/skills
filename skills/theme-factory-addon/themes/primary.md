# Primary

Soft and relaxing out of the box — warm cream with a friendly blue.

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#FAF8F4` - Base / background
- **Warm Gray**: `#ECE9E4` - Light neutral
- **Stone**: `#A8A29E` - Soft accent
- **Clay**: `#D98E73` - Mid-tone accent
- **Primary Blue**: `#3E7BFA` - Deep accent
- **Charcoal**: `#3B3A39` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: IBM Plex Mono (use IBM Plex Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: IBM Plex Sans

## Best Used For

Cozy personal knowledge bases, journaling apps, warm light decks.

> Category: Obsidian & Calm  ·  Mode: light
