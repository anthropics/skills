# Instagram

Instagram's signature sunset gradient from royal blue through pink to gold.

## Color Palette

- **Yellow**: `#FCAF45` - Base / background
- **Dark Orange**: `#F56040` - Light neutral
- **Royal Blue**: `#405DE6` - Soft accent
- **Purple-Red**: `#E1306C` - Mid-tone accent
- **Dark Pink**: `#C13584` - Deep accent
- **Purple**: `#833AB4` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Creator and social campaigns, vibrant marketing, lifestyle brands.
