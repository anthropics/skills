# Blue Topaz

A muted, relaxed blue-teal palette — calm and easy to live in.

## Color Palette

- **Mist**: `#EEF3F6` - Base / background
- **Sand**: `#D9E2E8` - Light neutral
- **Teal**: `#3AAFA9` - Soft accent
- **Topaz Blue**: `#2E86AB` - Mid-tone accent
- **Slate**: `#5D7A8C` - Deep accent
- **Ink**: `#2C3E50` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: IBM Plex Mono (use IBM Plex Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: IBM Plex Sans

## Best Used For

Research vaults, calm reading UIs, understated knowledge work.

> Category: Obsidian & Calm  ·  Mode: light
