# Spielberg

Warm nostalgic wonder — golden amber glow against deep teal night (E.T., Close Encounters).

## Color Palette

- **Night Teal**: `#13303A` - Base / background
- **Shadow Teal**: `#1F4A52` - Surface
- **Sky**: `#7FB0B8` - Muted accent
- **Amber Glow**: `#E0A23C` - Accent
- **Golden**: `#F2C879` - Bright accent
- **Cream Light**: `#F5E8C8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Heartfelt brand stories, family and adventure content, cinematic decks.
