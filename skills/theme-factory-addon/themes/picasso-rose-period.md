# Picasso Rose Period

The tender warmth of the Rose Period — circus pinks and terracotta over soft beige.

## Color Palette

- **Warm Beige**: `#E5D2B8` - Base / background
- **Soft Pink**: `#E8B7A6` - Light neutral
- **Rose**: `#D98C7A` - Soft accent
- **Blue-Gray**: `#8A9BA0` - Mid-tone accent
- **Terracotta**: `#C86B4A` - Deep accent
- **Clay Brown**: `#8A4B33` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Warm lifestyle brands, gentle editorial, romantic gallery layouts.
