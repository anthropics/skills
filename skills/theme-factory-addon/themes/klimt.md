# Klimt

The shimmer of The Kiss — gold leaf and jewel tones over velvet black.

## Color Palette

- **Black**: `#1A1610` - Base / background
- **Jewel Green**: `#4A6B4E` - Surface
- **Terracotta**: `#B5613A` - Muted accent
- **Deep Gold**: `#9A7B1E` - Accent
- **Gold Leaf**: `#C8A02C` - Bright accent
- **Ivory**: `#EDE2C4` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Luxury and beauty brands, opulent invitations, decorative gallery decks.
