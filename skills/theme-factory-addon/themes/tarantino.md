# Tarantino

Pulp-bold pop — Kill Bill yellow slashed with blood red and black.

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#F4ECCB` - Base / background
- **Kill Yellow**: `#FCE300` - Light neutral
- **Steel**: `#4A4A4A` - Soft accent
- **Blood Red**: `#B81414` - Mid-tone accent
- **Crimson**: `#7A0E0E` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#161210` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

High-energy brands, bold campaigns, attention-grabbing graphic decks.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: light
