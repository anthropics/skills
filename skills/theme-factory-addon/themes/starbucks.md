# Starbucks

Starbucks' siren green in deep and house-green tones.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Cream**: `#F1F8F5` - Light neutral
- **Starbucks Green**: `#00704A` - Accent
- **Accent Green**: `#006241` - Deep accent
- **House Green**: `#1E3932` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Cafe and hospitality, premium retail, warm lifestyle branding.
