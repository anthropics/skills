# Quiet Luxury

Understated grayscale with a soft mauve warmth.

## Color Palette

- **Soft White**: `#F2F2F2` - Base / background
- **Silver**: `#D9D9D9` - Light neutral
- **Mauve Gray**: `#BFAFAF` - Accent
- **Midnight**: `#0F1B27` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#0D0D0D` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Minimalist luxury, high-end fashion, restrained executive decks.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
