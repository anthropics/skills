# McDonalds

The golden arches: bright yellow and Mickey-D red on near-black.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Golden Yellow**: `#FFC72C` - Light accent
- **Mickey D Red**: `#DA291C` - Deep accent
- **Charcoal**: `#27251F` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Food and QSR brands, family-friendly campaigns, high-energy promos.
