# Modern Boho

Airy boho neutrals with a soft clay accent.

## Color Palette

- **Cloud**: `#EDEDED` - Base / background
- **Shell**: `#F5E8E0` - Light neutral
- **Mist**: `#E2E8E6` - Soft accent
- **Blush Clay**: `#EFC6B4` - Mid-tone accent
- **Tan**: `#D5B295` - Deep accent
- **Sage Gray**: `#9FB0AA` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Marcellus (use Marcellus Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Mulish

## Best Used For

Interiors and decor, slow-living brands, calm neutral decks.

> Category: Modern Boho & Neutrals  ·  Mode: light
