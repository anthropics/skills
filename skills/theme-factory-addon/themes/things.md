# Things

Inspired by the Things app — crisp white with a single confident blue.

## Color Palette

- **Paper**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Soft Gray**: `#EAECEF` - Light neutral
- **Things Blue**: `#0098FF` - Soft accent
- **Coral**: `#FF5A4D` - Mid-tone accent
- **Slate**: `#6E7178` - Deep accent
- **Ink**: `#1C1C1E` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: IBM Plex Mono (use IBM Plex Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: IBM Plex Sans

## Best Used For

Clean productivity apps, minimalist note UIs, focused light decks.

> Category: Obsidian & Calm  ·  Mode: light
