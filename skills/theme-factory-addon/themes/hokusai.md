# Hokusai

The crashing blues of The Great Wave — Prussian indigo against a pale sky.

## Color Palette

- **Foam White**: `#F2EFE6` - Base / background
- **Sky Cream**: `#E4D9B8` - Light neutral
- **Beige**: `#C9B68A` - Soft accent
- **Indigo**: `#2E5E8C` - Mid-tone accent
- **Prussian Blue**: `#1B3A6B` - Deep accent
- **Deep Navy**: `#14233F` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Travel and maritime brands, bold minimalist decks, Japandi-style design.

> Category: Master Painters  ·  Mode: light
