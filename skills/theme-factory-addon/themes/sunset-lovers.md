# Sunset Lovers

A warm romantic gradient that runs from peach glow to plum dusk at golden hour.

## Color Palette

- **Peach Glow**: `#FFEAD6` - Base / background
- **Golden Apricot**: `#FBC98E` - Light neutral
- **Coral Sunset**: `#F49A6E` - Soft accent
- **Burnt Coral**: `#E76F51` - Mid-tone accent
- **Raspberry Dusk**: `#B5485E` - Deep accent
- **Plum Twilight**: `#5C2A4D` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Nunito Sans

## Best Used For

Weddings and events, romance-led campaigns, warm invitations.

> Category: Feminine & Soft  ·  Mode: light
