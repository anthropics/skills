# Monet

Impressionist calm from Water Lilies — soft greens, mist, and floating mauve.

## Color Palette

- **Cream**: `#E8DCC0` - Base / background
- **Lily Mauve**: `#D6B8CE` - Light neutral
- **Willow**: `#A8C5B0` - Soft accent
- **Misty Blue**: `#6E9AA6` - Mid-tone accent
- **Lily Pink**: `#C76B8E` - Deep accent
- **Pond Teal**: `#2E5A6E` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Wellness and spa brands, soft lifestyle, serene editorial design.

> Category: Master Painters  ·  Mode: light
