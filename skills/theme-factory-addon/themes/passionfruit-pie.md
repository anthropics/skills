# Passionfruit Pie

Rich vintage warmth of brass, wine, and cocoa.

## Color Palette

- **Ivory**: `#F0EFEC` - Base / background
- **Pewter**: `#A9ACAD` - Light neutral
- **Brass**: `#B28735` - Soft accent
- **Chestnut**: `#866042` - Mid-tone accent
- **Wine**: `#702840` - Deep accent
- **Coffee**: `#412F26` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Playfair Display (use Playfair Display Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: EB Garamond

## Best Used For

Wineries and spirits, antique and craft brands, warm vintage layouts.
