# Salty Pink

Sea-softened pinks and mauves with a cool gray edge.

## Color Palette

- **Peach Salt**: `#F8D4C1` - Base / background
- **Shell Pink**: `#F0C4C2` - Light neutral
- **Rosewood**: `#D58C89` - Soft accent
- **Driftwood**: `#9C8C85` - Mid-tone accent
- **Mauve**: `#AE7F8A` - Deep accent
- **Slate Mauve**: `#6A6571` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Nunito Sans

## Best Used For

Coastal feminine brands, soft fashion, understated beauty content.

> Category: Feminine & Soft  ·  Mode: light
