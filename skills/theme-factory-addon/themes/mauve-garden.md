# Mauve Garden

Mauve and deep garden green for quiet drama.

## Color Palette

- **Oat**: `#D8C6BA` - Base / background
- **Mauve**: `#C09BAC` - Light neutral
- **Moss**: `#617246` - Accent
- **Pine**: `#314021` - Deep accent
- **Near Black**: `#0C0D07` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Floral and garden luxury, refined feminine brands, elegant decks.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
