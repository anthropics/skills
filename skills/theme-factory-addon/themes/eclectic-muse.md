# Eclectic Muse

Moody eclectic mix of camel, oxblood, and black.

## Color Palette

- **Eggshell**: `#FFFCF1` - Base / background
- **Peach**: `#F1CBB8` - Light neutral
- **Camel**: `#C9A475` - Soft accent
- **Olive Bronze**: `#6E5A2F` - Mid-tone accent
- **Oxblood**: `#56140F` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#000000` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Playfair Display (use Playfair Display Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: EB Garamond

## Best Used For

Art and gallery brands, vintage fashion, characterful editorial decks.

> Category: Vintage Romance  ·  Mode: light
