# Beautiful Blush

Layered blush tones from porcelain to terracotta rose.

## Color Palette

- **Porcelain**: `#FEEEE8` - Base / background
- **Petal**: `#F9D3D0` - Light neutral
- **Ash Rose**: `#C7BDBD` - Soft accent
- **Coral Blush**: `#F2A7A2` - Mid-tone accent
- **Clay Rose**: `#C98685` - Deep accent
- **Terracotta Rose**: `#C16B62` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Nunito Sans

## Best Used For

Beauty, skincare, lifestyle blogs, soft feminine product pages.

> Category: Feminine & Soft  ·  Mode: light
