# Fresh Fusion

Fresh, energetic blend of pink, gold, and blue.

## Color Palette

- **Rose**: `#F4CBD3` - Base / background
- **Honey**: `#F7CD9A` - Light neutral
- **Periwinkle**: `#B8D3EE` - Soft accent
- **Salmon**: `#EA9D9B` - Mid-tone accent
- **Teal**: `#599F9E` - Deep accent
- **Slate**: `#808285` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Poppins (use Poppins Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Modern lifestyle brands, food and drink, upbeat marketing decks.
