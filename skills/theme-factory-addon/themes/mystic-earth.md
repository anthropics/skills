# Mystic Earth

Soft mystic neutrals with warm tan and clay.

## Color Palette

- **Pale Gray**: `#D9D8DD` - Base / background
- **Mauve Mist**: `#D4C1C7` - Light neutral
- **Sand**: `#D8BFAB` - Soft accent
- **Camel**: `#E1BB94` - Mid-tone accent
- **Taupe**: `#9C8F89` - Deep accent
- **Walnut**: `#73604D` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Marcellus (use Marcellus Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Mulish

## Best Used For

Boho lifestyle brands, wellness, warm minimalist portfolios.

> Category: Modern Boho & Neutrals  ·  Mode: light
