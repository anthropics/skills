# Shades of Purple

A bold purple-forward theme with gold and cyan pops.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#2D2B55` - Base / background
- **Purple**: `#B362FF` - Surface
- **Pink**: `#FF628C` - Muted accent
- **Yellow**: `#FAD000` - Accent
- **Cyan**: `#9EFFFF` - Bright accent
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Mono (use Space Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Playful tech brands, creative coding, vibrant product showcases.

> Category: Neon & Retro  ·  Mode: dark
