# Rothko

Color-field intensity from Orange, Red, Yellow — molten reds glowing over warm dark.

## Color Palette

- **Warm Black**: `#1C1310` - Base / background
- **Maroon**: `#6E1A12` - Surface
- **Vermilion**: `#C8451B` - Muted accent
- **Amber**: `#D98B2B` - Accent
- **Marigold**: `#E8A22B` - Bright accent
- **Cream**: `#F2E3C6` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Bold gallery decks, emotive brand stories, dramatic editorial spreads.

> Category: Master Painters  ·  Mode: dark
