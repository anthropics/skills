# Tokyo Night

A downtown-at-night palette: deep indigo with neon blue and purple.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#1A1B26` - Base / background
- **Blue**: `#7AA2F7` - Surface
- **Purple**: `#BB9AF7` - Muted accent
- **Green**: `#9ECE6A` - Accent
- **Cyan**: `#7DCFFF` - Bright accent
- **Foreground**: `#C0CAF5` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: JetBrains Mono (use JetBrains Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Modern developer products, sleek dark dashboards, night-mode decks.
