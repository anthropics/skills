# Facebook

The classic Facebook blue with its clean grayscale interface tones.

## Color Palette

- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Base / background
- **Cloud Gray**: `#F0F2F5` - Light neutral
- **Facebook Blue**: `#1877F2` - Accent
- **Royal Blue**: `#4267B2` - Deep accent
- **Near Black**: `#1C1E21` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Social and community products, tech UIs, friendly corporate decks.
