# Nolan

Cold desaturated steel — gunmetal and slate blue, IMAX-scale and serious (Inception, Tenet).

## Color Palette

- **Gunmetal**: `#14181C` - Base / background
- **Slate**: `#2A333A` - Surface
- **Steel Blue**: `#46606E` - Muted accent
- **Cold Blue**: `#6E8A99` - Accent
- **Pale Steel**: `#A9BCC4` - Bright accent
- **Bone**: `#D8DEE0` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Tech and thriller brands, serious corporate decks, modern minimal UIs.
