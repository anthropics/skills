# Midnight Harbor

Inky navy warmed by sand and terracotta.

## Color Palette

- **Sand**: `#EBDED4` - Base / background
- **Apricot**: `#D9AA90` - Light neutral
- **Terracotta**: `#A65E46` - Accent
- **Navy**: `#07203F` - Deep accent
- **Ink**: `#02000D` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Sophisticated tech and finance, premium travel, confident corporate slides.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
