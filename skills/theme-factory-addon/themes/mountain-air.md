# Mountain Air

Crisp alpine air over deep pine and slate — grounded yet open.

## Color Palette

- **Glacier Mist**: `#D4E1E7` - Base / background
- **Sky Wash**: `#99BAD7` - Light neutral
- **Sage**: `#7F9A83` - Soft accent
- **Moss**: `#778C58` - Mid-tone accent
- **Slate Teal**: `#254B5A` - Deep accent
- **Deep Spruce**: `#082434` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Fraunces (use Fraunces Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Karla

## Best Used For

Outdoor and sustainability brands, wellness retreats, calm nature-led decks.

> Category: Earthy & Grounded  ·  Mode: light
