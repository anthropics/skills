# Everforest

A soft, low-contrast forest palette — sage green and warm gold on deep pine.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#2D353B` - Base / background
- **Orange**: `#E69875` - Surface
- **Aqua**: `#83C092` - Muted accent
- **Green**: `#A7C080` - Accent
- **Yellow**: `#DBBC7F` - Bright accent
- **Foreground**: `#D3C6AA` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: IBM Plex Mono (use IBM Plex Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: IBM Plex Sans

## Best Used For

Calm note-taking UIs, wellness-tech, gentle low-contrast documentation.

> Category: Obsidian & Calm  ·  Mode: dark
