# Gordon Willis

The 'Prince of Darkness' — golden amber emerging from near-black shadow (The Godfather).

## Color Palette

- **Shadow Black**: `#120D08` - Base / background
- **Deep Brown**: `#2E1F12` - Surface
- **Sepia**: `#5A3E22` - Muted accent
- **Amber**: `#9A6A2A` - Accent
- **Golden Ochre**: `#C89A48` - Bright accent
- **Warm Ivory**: `#E6D2A8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Heritage luxury, prestige brand storytelling, low-key dramatic decks.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: dark
