# Slate Mist

Tranquil slate blues with a clean off-white.

## Color Palette

- **Mist White**: `#F3F5F9` - Base / background
- **Off White**: `#F2F2F0` - Light neutral
- **Slate Blue**: `#819FA7` - Accent
- **Deep Slate**: `#5B6E74` - Deep accent
- **Black**: `#0D0D0D` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Calm professional brands, wellness-luxury, understated reports.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
