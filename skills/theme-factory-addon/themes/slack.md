# Slack

Slack's aubergine with its playful four-color accent set.

## Color Palette

- **Yellow**: `#ECB22E` - Base / background
- **Blue**: `#36C5F0` - Light neutral
- **Green**: `#2EB67D` - Accent
- **Red**: `#E01E5A` - Deep accent
- **Aubergine**: `#4A154B` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat (use Montserrat Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Work Sans

## Best Used For

Productivity and SaaS, playful B2B brands, collaborative product decks.
