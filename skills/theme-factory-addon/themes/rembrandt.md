# Rembrandt

Chiaroscuro drama — golden light emerging from deep umber shadow.

## Color Palette

- **Near Black**: `#1A140E` - Base / background
- **Umber**: `#3D2B1A` - Surface
- **Oxblood**: `#6E2018` - Muted accent
- **Raw Sienna**: `#8A5A22` - Accent
- **Golden Ochre**: `#C68A3A` - Bright accent
- **Ivory**: `#E8D5A8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Cormorant Garamond (use Cormorant Garamond Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Spectral

## Best Used For

Luxury heritage brands, museum and gallery decks, refined dark layouts.

> Category: Master Painters  ·  Mode: dark
