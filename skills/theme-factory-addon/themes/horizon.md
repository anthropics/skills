# Horizon

A warm sunset-on-dark palette — rose and coral against deep charcoal.

## Color Palette

- **Background**: `#1C1E26` - Base / background
- **Rose**: `#E95678` - Surface
- **Purple**: `#B877DB` - Muted accent
- **Coral**: `#FAB795` - Accent
- **Cyan**: `#59E1E3` - Bright accent
- **Foreground**: `#D5D8DA` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Space Mono (use Space Mono Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Inter

## Best Used For

Warm-toned dev brands, creative tools, stylish dark presentations.
