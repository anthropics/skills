# Warm Linen

Refined warm neutrals from linen to chestnut.

## Color Palette

- **Ivory**: `#F2F1EF` - Base / background
- **Linen**: `#D9D2CC` - Light neutral
- **Sand**: `#D9B18E` - Accent
- **Clay**: `#A67564` - Deep accent
- **Chestnut**: `#723E31` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Bodoni Moda (use Bodoni Moda Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Montserrat

## Best Used For

Luxury neutrals branding, interiors, premium minimalist decks.

> Category: Luxury & Sophisticated  ·  Mode: light
