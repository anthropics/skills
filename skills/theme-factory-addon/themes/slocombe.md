# Slocombe

Golden adventure glow — rich ochre and warm brown of the Indiana Jones trilogy.

## Color Palette

- **Jungle Shadow**: `#1C1A10` - Base / background
- **Deep Brown**: `#3A2A16` - Surface
- **Olive**: `#6B5A2A` - Muted accent
- **Ochre**: `#B5852E` - Accent
- **Golden**: `#E0A93C` - Bright accent
- **Warm Sand**: `#EAD7A8` - Foreground / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Oswald (use Oswald Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Barlow

## Best Used For

Adventure and travel brands, heritage storytelling, warm epic decks.

> Category: Directors & Cinematographers  ·  Mode: dark
