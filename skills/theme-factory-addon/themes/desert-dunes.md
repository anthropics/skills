# Desert Dunes

Sun-baked sand and rich clay for warm, earthy storytelling.

## Color Palette

- **Dune Sand**: `#E9DECE` - Base / background
- **Wheat**: `#E6CDA9` - Light neutral
- **Amber Clay**: `#9D5F1B` - Soft accent
- **Cedar**: `#6C3C0C` - Mid-tone accent
- **Burnt Sienna**: `#722A07` - Deep accent
- **Espresso**: `#291602` - Anchor / text

## Typography

- **Headers**: Fraunces (use Fraunces Bold for emphasis)
- **Body Text**: Karla

## Best Used For

Artisan and heritage brands, travel and hospitality, rustic product launches.

> Category: Earthy & Grounded  ·  Mode: light
